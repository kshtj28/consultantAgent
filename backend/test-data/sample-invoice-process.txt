# Sample Business Process Document for Testing

## Invoice Processing Workflow

### Current State

The finance team currently processes approximately 500 invoices per month using the following manual workflow:

1. **Invoice Receipt**: Invoices arrive via email or physical mail
2. **Data Entry**: Finance clerk manually enters invoice data into Excel spreadsheet
3. **Validation**: Manager reviews entries for accuracy
4. **Approval**: Department head approves invoices over $5,000
5. **Payment**: Treasury team schedules payment in banking system
6. **Filing**: Physical copies are scanned and stored

### Pain Points

- Manual data entry takes 15-20 minutes per invoice
- High error rate (approximately 8% require rework)
- Approval delays due to manager availability
- No real-time visibility into invoice status
- Duplicate payment risk due to manual tracking

### Stakeholders

- **Finance Team**: Primary users of the invoice system
- **Department Heads**: Approve large invoices
- **Vendors**: Submit invoices and expect timely payment
- **Treasury**: Execute payments

### Systems Used

- Microsoft Excel for tracking
- Outlook for email communication
- Banking portal for payments
- SharePoint for document storage

### Metrics

| Metric | Current Value | Target |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| Processing Time | 3-5 days | 1 day |
| Error Rate | 8% | <1% |
| Approval Cycle | 2-3 days | 4 hours |
| Late Payments | 12% | 0% |
